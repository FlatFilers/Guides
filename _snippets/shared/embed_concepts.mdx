Front-end SDK wrappers around the [Flatfile API](https://reference.flatfile.com/) enable you to effortlessly and securely integrate an embedded data import experience directly into your application.

## What to expect

### Time to complete

Seeing an instance of Flatfile embedded inside an application only takes about 5 minutes to complete.

### What you'll learn

This quickstart guide will show you how to:

- Seamlessly embed Flatfile within your platform
- Set up a simple Workbook as a jumping off point that you can later match to your schema.
- Implement transformations and validations on the data you receive.
- Customize the importer's theme to match your brand colors.
- Configure a destination to submit data via webhook.

Once complete, you'll find many additional options to customize Flatfile specifically for your use case.
