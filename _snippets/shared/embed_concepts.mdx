Front-end SDK wrappers around the [Flatfile API](https://reference.flatfile.com/docs/api/) enable you to effortlessly and securely integrate an embedded data import experience directly into your application.

By the end of the quickstart guide (should only take you a few minutes), you will be able to:

- Set up a Workbook and Sheets that align with your schema.
- Implement transformations and validations on your data.
- Customize the importer's theme to match your brand.
- Configure a destination to submit data.
- And finally, seamlessly embed Flatfile within your platform.

There are also many advanced options you can learn about to customize Flatfile for your use case.
