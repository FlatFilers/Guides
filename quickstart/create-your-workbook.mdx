api: "POST https://platform.flatfile.com/api/v1/workbooks"
---

<Tip>
  The Bearer token you will need for this call is your unique [secret
  key](../developer-tools/security/authentication#secret-and-publishable-keys).
  It can be found in the Developer settings of your Flatfile account [here
  ](https://platform.flatfile.com/developers).
</Tip>
