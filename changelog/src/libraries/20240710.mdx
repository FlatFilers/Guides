      `@flatfile/art@0.0.1`

      This package now provides three different ASCII art designs that can be randomly printed to the console when the `art` binary is executed.

      Two new art functions have been added: `printFlowerArt` and `printMusicArt`. 
      The `printFlowerArt` function prints a flower design to the console. 
      The `printMusicArt` function prints a musical design to the console and the quote "If music be the food of love play on!".
